Triple Decker Brownie Recipie

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Press the cookie dough in an even layer on the bottom of a baking dish
    Place the cookies in an even layer on the top of the cookie dough
    Pour thr brownie batter on top, spreading it evenly over the cookies
  3. Bake for 45-50 minutes
